Identity Guidelines

future//of>>tech demystifies the technology we encounter in our everyday lives, knowingly or unknowingly.

It helps us deepen our understanding of technology in meaningful and useful ways. Ways that allow us to…

be active participants in shaping the future of technology,

be thoughtful in how we engage with technology,

consider how technology will impact our careers,

consider how technology will impact our world.

Accessibility: AA Contrast

Do not set text in an accent color or in white text on top of an accent color, unless that text is a decorative element. The big, light yellow section numbers on this page are an example of decorative text. Light slate is accessible on white but does not contrast sufficiently with dark slate.

Pass: AA or AAA contrast
Fail: only decorative text
